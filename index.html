Tips & Comfort

  • 💳 Contactless everywhere; keep a few $1 bills for tips.
  • 💁‍♀️ Tipping: 18–20% restaurants; taxis round-up; some places auto-add gratuity.
  • 📱 Apps: Google Maps, Uber/Lyft, CitiBike. OMNY works with your card/phone at the subway gates.
  • 👟 Expect 10k+ steps/day. Weather late-Sep ~20–25°C; evenings cooler. Light jacket & comfy shoes.
  • 🩺 GF note: Most places above can do GF (bun, pasta, or separate prep). When in doubt, ask.
  • 🆘 If stuck: Call Sam first (+44 7545 639 420).
